Public engagement

Bringing the law I work on to audiences beyond the university.

Open access

I have used an open channel on Twitch (twitch.tv/lukasrassmasson) to teach and discuss law in the open. I began streaming my courses in September 2020, on my own initiative and at my own expense: my aim was to let Toulouse students follow my teaching while on mobility, and to use the platform's interactivity with large first-year cohorts, alongside guest conferences left available in replay to students, enthusiasts, and practitioners alike. A short presentation of the channel remains online. The channel is not currently active.

I am considering resuming it around a simple idea: making private international law, and international law more broadly, freely accessible, potentially extending to comparative law and French civil law.
