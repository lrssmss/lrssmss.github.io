Research areas

Private international law and its methodology; European private international law; comparative private international law (in particular between Europe and Southeast Asia); the law of the global commons and global spaces (cyberspace, outer space); law and new technologies, including artificial intelligence and law; legal humanism and decolonial approaches; the rule of law and transitional justice.
